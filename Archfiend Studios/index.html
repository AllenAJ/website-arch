About Us

Welcome to the exciting world of stunt riding! Stunt Bike Freestyle, our first venture is a daring adventure that combines passion for gaming and stunt riding. Brain child of stunt riders turned game developers, it is one of the first games to focus entirely on stunt riding. The game is built with the intention of providing the ultimate stunt riding experience to the gamer, and is uniquely equipped with features like bike customization and even performance tuning. Here are a few ways in which the gamer can co-create the aesthetic feel of the ride and the bike that is fit for their personality or their temperment. *cut and crop parts to change the shape and look of the bike *install or remove parts that help in performance and visuals *paint different parts of your bike like fairing, frame, cage, swing arm, handle,sub cage, blah, blah, blah...Frankly, the list is quite long. An impressive 2.5 million downloads in google play store alone, a total 3.5 million in all other platforms together, we continue to get rave reviews and garner postive feedback. Made exclusively for mobile platforms, it is available at all popular platforms including Android and iOS. Actually its only available on iOS and Android. This is a call for the rider hidden within you. Stunt Bike Freestyle awaits you!
